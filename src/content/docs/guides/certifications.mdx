Here is a list of popular cloud certifications that can help you advance your career in cloud computing. These certifications are offered by leading cloud service providers and cover a range of topics, from foundational knowledge to advanced skills in specific areas. 

For novices, we recommend that you pursue a foundational certification before moving on to core and specialized certifications.

## List of certifications

<Aside type="note" title="Keep in mind" icon="starlight">
We have included mostly certifications from AWS and Kubernetes. For a more comprehensive list, refer to the [certifications](https://skillspathway.scs.org.sg/cloud/courses-certifications/) listed on the skills pathway by the Singapore Computer Society.
</Aside>

### Foundational certifications
1. AWS Certified Cloud Practitioner <Badge text="recommended" variant="success" />

### Core certifications
1. AWS Certified Solutions Architect – Associate <Badge text="recommended" variant="success" />
1. Kubernetes and Cloud Native Associate (KCNA) 

### Specialization certifications
1. AWS Certified Security – Specialty
1. AWS Certified DevOps Engineer Professional
1. Certified Kubernetes Administrator (CKA)

## Learning resources

1. [LinkedIn Learning](https://www.linkedin.com/learning/) – You can access courses that can help you prepare for various certifications. 
1. Free access to LinkedIn Learning with an [NLB](https://eresources.nlb.gov.sg/main/linkedin.html) account – For those of you who have access to an NLB account, you can access LinkedIn Learning for free.
1. [Tutorials Dojo](https://tutorialsdojo.com/) – For practice exams.
1. [Stephane Maarek](https://www.udemy.com/user/stephane-maarek/)'s courses on udemy – His courses on AWS certifications are highly rated.
